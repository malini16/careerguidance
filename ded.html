Diploma in Education (D.Ed.)

D.Ed. course can be pursued by students who have passed 10+2 (any stream) from a recognized board. The course has been designed to improve the elementary level teacher workforce in India. D.Ed. course caters to the needs of elementary level education segment.

D.Ed. course is technically an elementary teacher training course. Diploma in Education is similar to D.El.Ed. (Diploma in Elementary Education), JBT, D.T.Ed. (Diploma in Teachers Education) and ETT (Elementary Teacher Training) courses. D.Ed., JBT, D.El.Ed., D.T.Ed. and ETT courses have similar syllabus and course structure. These courses also offer similar job opportunities.

After completing 2 years long academic program, D.Ed. professionals become eligible to work at elementary level schools, nurseries and similar educational institutes imparting elementary education. This teacher training course suits those who want to teach at lower (elementary) level schools.

DIPLOMA IN EDUCATION (D.ED.): COURSE DETAILS

The table provided below covers the basic details about D.Ed. course –

OVERVIEW

Course Name: Diploma in Education (D.Ed.)
Course Duration: 2 years
Eligibility Criteria: 10+2 passed

TYPE OF COURSE

As the name suggests, it is a Diploma program. The course has been designed to impart teaching skills, teaching methodology and subject-wise knowledge to candidates.

DURATION

The academic program is 2 years long.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Minimum qualification required is 10+2 passed (any stream) from a recognized board with at least 50% aggregate marks.

ADMISSION PROCESS

Merit based admission process in cases of reputed institutes. Deserving candidates are selected through entrance test, selection process, and/or on the basis of their performance in board examination.

SYLLABUS

Here are some important subjects present in the D.Ed. academic program. This list will give you a basic idea about the course’s structure and blueprint –

  • Child Development and Learning
  • Curriculum and Pedagogy
  • Regional Language
  • English Language Teaching
  • Environmental Science Teaching
  • Mathematics Teaching
  • Art Education
  • Physical Education
  • Literature
  • Social Science Teaching
  • General Science Education

CAREER PROSPECTS AND JOB OPPORTUNITIES

As I mentioned before, the main aim of D.Ed. course is to enhance and develop the Nation’s elementary teacher workforce. D.Ed. course churns out skilled and qualified elementary education teachers each year.

After completing Diploma in Education course, successful candidates may work as elementary level teachers at educational institutes. They may find work at institutes imparting elementary level education, like –

  • Government schools
  • Private schools
  • Nursery
  • Coaching/Private tuition classes
  • Day care centres

In the above mentioned work setups, D.Ed. professionals may don roles such as –

  • Teacher
  • Assistant Teacher
  • Tutor
  • Student Counselor

D.Ed. professionals are also eligible to start their own nursery or day care centre. Apart from that, private coaching and tuition classes are other forms of self employment available in front of them.

D.Ed. is a job oriented teacher training course. If you are interested in teaching at elementary level, this course is for you! You may join the course right after completing 12th. If you are interested in teaching at a higher level, you may check out this list of teacher training courses.
